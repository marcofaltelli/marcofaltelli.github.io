Bio

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Rome Tor Vergata. My work is under the supervision of Prof. Giuseppe Bianchi and Prof. Francesco Quaglia. My research activities combine computer networking and computer architecture to design high performing, scalable architectures for both network hardware and software solutions. In October 2019 I received my Master's Degree in Computer Engineering cum laude at the University of Rome Tor Vergata.
I am also collaborating with CNIT on different European projects (currently LOCUS, 5G-PICTURE in the past).
I further collaborate with Axbryd, a startup company founded from the NetProg group of the University of Rome "Tor Vergata".
